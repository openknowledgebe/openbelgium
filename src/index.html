March 2021

Open Belgium is an annual community event, where we make Belgian knowledge open, usable, useful and used.

Speakers

The open community gets a say in our program!
The Call for Speakers is an open invitation to government officials, researchers,
industry influencers and open enthusiasts to share their experiences on open data
and open knowledge during a Q&A session, a roundtable discussion or workshop.
Submit your proposal before the 31th of January 2021!

The line-up will be announced in the beginning of February

Sessions

Since COVID19 has limited our contacts dramatically during the past year,
we want to involve our community as much as possible during Open Belgium.
That is why we choose to focus on interaction with the audience during Q&A sessions,
roundtable discussions and workshops. These online sessions will be spread out over the entire month
of March to give our community the chance to participate in every session
without causing video conference exhaustion.

Stay tuned, more information will follow shortly
